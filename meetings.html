Monthly Meetings

The Edmonton Reptile & Amphibian Society always meets the 3rd Tuesday of every month @ 7:30pm except for December (due to the holidays ERAS meets on the 2nd Tuesday in December). The monthly meetings are comprised of discussing society business, a meet and greet, show & tell for those who bring pets and guest speakers/special presentations. Please feel free to drop in to find out more about ERAS!

Every month will have a pre-set theme based on various species of our favourite critters. This theme will coincide with a member-based presentation on the animal being showcased, and you are ALL encouraged to bring your pet that follows the theme. We will dedicate a portion of each meeting towards discussion of the care, health and husbandry of the species that is being featured at that meeting.
Check the forums or the homepage before the monthly meeting to see what the monthly theme is.

Can I bring my animals?

YES! Yes, you can!

Bringing your reptiles, amphibians and invertebrates to show at our meetings is greatly encouraged so long as a few important considerations are met.

First, all sick animals must be left at home! Second, new pets should be kept in quarantine at home to observe for illness to ensure they are not sick. (ie. animals purchased at the ERAS Fall Show & Sale should not be brought to the October/November meetings.) And finally priority needs to be placed on maintaining proper care during transportation; especially during colder weather!

(Note: Animals are not allowed to be brought to the December meeting because of the Christmas potluck dinner.)
