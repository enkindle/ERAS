ERAS NEWSLETTER • The Herp Digest

The Edmonton Reptile & Amphibian Society published a quarterly newsletter every year made that was made available to society members. The newsletter was called The Herp Digest (Herp is a vernacular term for reptiles and amphibians derived from the old term Herpetile).

The newsletter was made available to all ERAS Members four times a year and was distributed electronically in stunning full-colour via the internet using Adobe's Portable Document Format (PDF).

The Herp Digest contained current information related to on-going society business, but was primarily composed of various member-generated articles and photographs such as pet caresheets, rescue stories, ERAS event coverage, and field-herping stories.

ERAS NEWSLETTER • How do I contribute?

The newsletter is no longer being produced, but in the coming months you can look forward to past issues being uploaded to this site. These will be made available for everyone to view and download.
