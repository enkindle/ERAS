ERAS CONTACT INFORMATION

GENERAL INPUT

At any time, any member or non-member can contact an executive of their choosing with input, advice, criticism, or ideas on how to make ERAS better. This society is for you, and we are all here to help ERAS meet it's mandate of education, conservation and research. The biggest part of this mandate is ensuring the membership and public have an accessible, informative, and friendly atmosphere to enjoy and learn about their reptiles and amphibians.

COMPLAINTS POLICY

ERAS has in place a policy to deal with complaints and other issues. If you have a complaint or concern about ERAS or an ERAS member, it should be forwarded to the president where it will be documented and discussed amongst the executive to find an appropriate resolution. If you do not feel comfortable approaching the president, then approach any other member of the executive.

This policy is in place to keep the forums and meetings a positive and friendly place to be and protects ERAS and it's members. If need be, once the issue has been appropriately investigated it may be put on the agenda for discussion at a general meeting. All complaints are recorded to identify repeat offenders.
