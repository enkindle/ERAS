ADOPTING from the RESCUE - PLEASE READ!

While we strive to ensure the list below is as up-to-date as possible it may not always reflect the most recent incoming surrenders or outgoing adoptions. Please check with the Edmonton Reptile Rehab & Rescue directly for the most up-to-date availability list.

If you are interested in adopting an animal listed below and would like to enquire about its availability or simply to see what else may be available please contact the RESCUE Coordinator via email at rescue@edmontonreptiles.com (Note: Animals will not be adopted out via email contact alone as phone & in-person contact is required)

You may also call the ERAS Rescue Line at 780-429-0934 and leave a message with a contact number and your call will be returned within 1-3 days. If there is a certain species you are looking for you can request to have your name added to the waiting list so that you may be contacted in the event that what you are looking for is surrendered in the future.

Please keep in mind that you must demonstrate knowledge of the proper care required for the animal you are interested in to be considered during the application process as a new home and that a small adoption fee will be required determined on an individual animal basis.

THANK YOU for considering adopting an animal from Edmonton's unique ERAS supported Reptile RESCUE!

ADOPTION DISCLAIMER:

The Edmonton Reptile & Amphibian Society and the Reptile Rescue reserves the right to refuse any adoption request for reasons to be kept private and confidential within the administration of the organization.
